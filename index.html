World Athletics Recruits

World Athletes Recruits (W.A.R.) is a recruitment/scouting agency offering a service to link prospective student athletes with collegiate sporting coaches in the United States of America. We are dedicated to our unwavering passion and desire to see student athletes excel and be exposed. Our services are entirely influenced by the needs, visions and ambitions of prospective African/International students
